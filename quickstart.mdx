## Quickstart

Set up your janek unon documentation workspace in minutes. Follow these steps to create an account, configure your space, and upload your first content. No prior experience required.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Complete the entire process in under 5 minutes. Use the CLI for automation or the web interface for simplicity.
</Callout>

## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account" icon="user-plus">

    Sign up at the janek unon dashboard.

    ```bash
    npx unon@latest auth signup --email="your-email@example.com" --password="secure-password"
    ```

    Or visit [https://app.janek-unon.com/signup](https://app.janek-unon.com/signup) in your browser and follow the prompts.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Workspace" icon="folder-plus">

    Initialize a new documentation workspace tied to your account.

    ````bash
    npx unon@latest workspace create my-project-docs \
      --description="Documentation for my project" \
      --brand-color="#3B82F6"
    ````

    Note your workspace ID: `ws_abc123def456`. Save it for future commands.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload and Organize Initial Content" icon="upload-cloud">

    Add your first Markdown files to the workspace.

    Use the CLI to upload a directory:

    ````bash
    npx unon@latest upload ./docs --workspace="ws_abc123def456" \
      --structure="quickstart.mdx:./quickstart.mdx,api.mdx:./api/"
    ````

    Organize content into folders like `guides/`, `api/`, and `changelog.mdx`.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Methods

Choose your preferred method to add content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Drag-and-Drop" icon="mouse-pointer">

    1. Navigate to your workspace dashboard.
    2. Drag files or folders into the upload zone.
    3. Content auto-organizes by filename.

    <Image
      src="https://example.com/docs-drag-drop.png"
      alt="Drag and drop files into the workspace upload area"
      width="800"
      height="500"
    />

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CLI" icon="terminal">

    <CodeGroup tabs="Bash,PowerShell">
      ````bash
      npx unon@latest upload ./src/docs --workspace="ws_abc123def456"
      ````

      ````powershell
      unon upload ./src/docs --workspace "ws_abc123def456"
      ````
    </CodeGroup>

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Git Integration" icon="git-branch">

    Connect your GitHub repo directly.

    1. In dashboard, go to Integrations > GitHub.
    2. Authorize and select `your-org/project-docs`.
    3. Sync publishes changes automatically.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
